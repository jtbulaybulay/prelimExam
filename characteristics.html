Characteristics of BS CpE Graduates

  • Possess the ability to design computer, computer-based systems, and networks that include both hardware and software and their integration to solve novel engineering problems.
  • Have a breadth of kwowledge in mathematics and engineering sciences, associated with the broader scope of engineering and beyong that narrowly required for the field.
