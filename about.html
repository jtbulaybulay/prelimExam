WHAT ARE THE CAREER OPPORTUNITIES OF BECOMING A COMPUTER ENGINEER?

SOFTWARE ENGINEER

research, design and write new software programs (e.g. business applications or computer games) and computer operating systems. evaluate the software and systems that make computers and hardware work. develop existing programs by analysing and identifying areas for modification.

HARDWARE ENGINEER

Computer hardware engineers research, design, develop, and test computer systems and components such as circuit boards. Computer hardware engineers research, design, develop, and test computer systems and components such as processors, circuit boards, memory devices, networks, and routers.

click to know more
ELECTRICAL ENGINEER

Electrical engineers design, develop and maintain electrical systems for buildings, transport systems and power distribution networks. They work in and across many industries, such as construction, transport, energy (including renewables), building services, and manufacturing.

click to know more
NETWORK ENGINEER

Network Engineers primarily handle installing, configuring, and supporting firewalls to ensure network security. They are also responsible for procuring equipment like routers or switches, which provides the best possible efficiency in an office's IT infrastructure.

click to know more
COMPUTER SCIENCE ENGINEER

Computer engineers evaluate, design, and maintain computer hardware and software systems. They develop, test, and design, computer processors, circuit boards, and network systems. They resolve problems or address any concerns that occur with the hardware or software and aid in the advancement of computer technology.
