WHAT IS COMPUTER ENGINEERING?

Computer engineering is a four-year engineering program that embodies the science and technology of design, development, implementation, maintenance and integration of software and hardware components in modern computing systems and computer-controlled equipment.

Field of Specializations

SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT

Software development refers to a set of computer science activities dedicated to the process of creating, designing, deploying, and supporting software.

Want to know more?
ROBOTICS

Robotics is a branch of engineering that involves the conception, design, manufacture and operation of robots. The objective of the robotics field is to create intelligent machines that can assist humans in a variety of ways.

Want to know more?
NETWORKS and SYSTEMS ADMINISTRATIONS

A network computer administrator maintains, installs, and organizes the networks of local area networks (LAN), wide area networks (WAN) and intranets.

Want to know more?
EMBEDDED SYSTEMS

An embedded system is a microprocessor-based computer hardware system with software that is designed to perform a dedicated function, either as an independent system or as a part of a large system. At the core is an integrated circuit designed to carry out computation for real-time operations.
